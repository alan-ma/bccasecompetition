Hosted by FUSE Society | SFU Vancouver Harbour Center | June 10 2017

Calling all high school students & aspiring business leaders!
The FUSE Case Competition will take place on June 10th, 2017 at SFU Harbour Centre from 9:00AM to 5:30PM. With over 100 high school students in attendance, delegates can expect an action-packed day focused on entrepreneurship and consulting. Each team will create a unique company based on a theme (TBA) and will then be challenged to overcome company crises, including a PR crisis.
REGISTRATION DEADLINE: JUNE 3rd at 11:59PM.

Keynote Speaker: Hussein Hallak

FUSE Society is proud to present Hussein Hallak as the Keynote Speaker for the 2017 FUSE Case Competition.

With 25+ years of experience in startups, strategy, management, execution, branding, marketing, and growth, Hussein has worked on major projects for Fortune 500 companies and World's Top 100 Brands. As General Manager and Head Mentor at Launch Academy, Western Canada’s leading tech startup hub with more than 500 startups incubated, he has raised over $100 million and mentored more than 1000 entrepreneurs.
He is the Executive Director at TiE Vancouver, Instructor at Capilano University, and Tech Panelist on Roundhouse Radio. He served as the former Director of Marketing at TEDxStanleyPark; one of the world’s largest and most successful TEDx events. He was the former Director of Learning & Development of BNI Middle East, the world’s largest business networking organization with over 200,000 members worldwide, and the founder and CEO of INFIN8VENTURES, an award-winning communications agency that won the prestigious SNCR Excellence in New Communications Award for Social Media Marketing alongside UBC and Adobe.
Built 20+ startups. Mentored and trained 1000+ entrepreneurs. Featured in Forbes, BBC, Entrepreneur, DailyHive (VancityBuzz), Business In Vancouver, Roundhouse Radio, and more. Excited? Sign up for the Case Competition today.
